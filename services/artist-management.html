ARTIST MANAGEMENT

Artist management involves the comprehensive guidance, support, and strategic planning provided by a professional manager or management team to an artist, musician, or performer to advance their career. This encompasses a wide range of responsibilities, including career development, negotiation of contracts, scheduling, promotion, financial management, and overall advocacy on behalf of the artist, with the ultimate goal of helping them achieve their artistic and commercial objectives while navigating the complexities of the entertainment industry.
